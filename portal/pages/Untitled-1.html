Overview:
In order to promote Bhopal as a startup city, we are going to organize Startup Month with National Entrepreneurship Network NEN. “Startup Month” is an inter college business plan competition to increase, motivate and encourage students for startup. We train teams full month by providing different field task related to selling, marketing, networking, financial projections, case study experience to field experts and stories of other startups.
Schedule:

  • Campaign and registration starts from 1st September 2016 to 30th October 2016
  • Learning Month starts from 2nd November
    • First seminar will conduct on 6th November and first field task will allot.
    • Second seminar will conduct on 13th November and second field task will allot.
    • Third field task will allot on 16th oct
    • Third seminar will conduct on 27th nov and start of competition
  • Qualify teams will upload their presentation from 27th November to 4th December 2016
  • Round 2 will begin on 10th December
    • Round 1 qualify team will show their presentation at Bhopal- M.P.
    • Top 10 teams will select in round 2
  • 13th December- Announcement of Winners and Awards Ceremony
